We experienced the limitations of existing market solutions firsthand and decided to build the platform that actually solves the real-world challenges faced by clubs and players.
Now, we are moving away from an external development agency to build a dedicated internal core team. We need a founding engineer to join us at an early stage—not just to write code, but to help shape architecture, product decisions, and the future direction of the platform.
Our long-term goal is to become the leading booking and community platform for racket sports clubs worldwide.
